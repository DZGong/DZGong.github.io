Multi-scan 3D-SIM

The gDMD-SIM offers a general solution to the multi-color DMD SIM challenge. Although being widely used for SIM pattern genration due to their high speed & low cost, DMD's blazed grating structure causes strong wavelength-dependent dispersion, thus severly hampering multi-color imaging application. The gDMD-SIM system employs an additional blazed grating to compensate the dispersion, and can easily allow for multi-color SIM imaging with a wide range of excitation beams with minimal effort.
