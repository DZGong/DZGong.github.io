Multi-scan 3D-SIM

High-speed 3D-SIM achieved using a multiplane image splitter for simultaneous imaging cross multiple planes, complemented by a deformable mirror (DM) for rapid remote focusing and aberration correction.

Figure: up, fluorescent beads monolayer imaged by WF vs 3D-SIM. bottom, animation of the multi-scan 3D-SIM.

Virtual multi-channel imaging

μSplit, a convolutional neural network model was adapted and capable of 3 channels demixing for SIM imaging. The novel μSplit-SIM model allows for virtual 3-channel SIM imaging within only 1 exposure time and 1 color label.

Figure: animation of the performance of virtual multi-channel imaging.

Snapshot compressive imaging SIM

Combinning with Snapshot Compressive Imaging (SCI), an image reconstruction algorithm is developed to reconstruct a super-resolution image from the single acquisition of the SCI-SIM image.

Figure: performance of the SCI-SIM reconstruction.
